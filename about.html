More About Karamel Co. & The Baker

Back in June 2015, Amelia started out with her first ever bakesale as Amebakes, selling classic flavours like Oreo Choco, Matcha White Choco and Biscoff Speculoos. Back then, it was just a spark of interest that kicked things up - and it became a hit. Then on, she started cracking her brains for willy wacky flavours, or coming up flavours the crowd would love. It was not so long after, she felt that something was missing.

We've neglected what it's like to take a step at a time. Everyone's chasing, catching up with trends but it slipped our minds that there are actually trends worth rewinding back - just like oldies worth reminiscing - classics.

After months of brainstorming, finally - In February 2017, Karamel Co. has been launched.

Inspired by what versatile ingredients like sugar could do, Amelia decided to re-embark this humble journey by exploring flavours that complement not just caramel, but the classics we all enjoy, and what brought her this far.

In the midst of keeping up with newest trends, we would love to rewind a track back where you can enjoy the rhythm to your blessings. Slowly, steadily but surely, we promise to pace ourselves, providing the best series and tracks - always.

Amelia is currently working on her own - baking and creating for your extra special occasions, answering enquiries, delivering, and doing the dishes.

Most importantly, Amelia wants to express gratitude for the love and support since the start.
