Made to Order

We offer a range of cupcakes & bakes to satisfy your bellies and make your celebration extra exciting!

All baked goods are made to order, from scratch in small batches. We aim to create and combine classic flavours with new and interesting taste, all while using the finest quality ingredients – fresh eggs from local supplier, pure Madagascar Bourbon vanilla, 55% Belgium Chocolate and more!

A gallery of our recent cupcake designs can be found on our Instagram page @karamelco.

Packaging

Just as much as we take pride in what we bake, our packaging is important to us. Every baked good is packed in its own box - box of 6 or 12, complete with a complimentary gift card. We hope that every receiver would love our detailed packaging. That being said, any special requests on packaging may be subject to additional charges.

Packaging available in kraft boxes - individual, boxes of 2, 4, 6 and 12.
Feel free to drop us an email if you have any preferred packaging!
