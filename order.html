Notes upon Ordering:

•   We recommend placing an order with us as early as possible. If you’re unsure when is the specific date, do provide us with the week that you want to place an order.

•   For cupcakes, available in multiples of 6. Minimum order is half dozen.

•   No flavour restrictions - You can mix and match all flavours.

•   Full deposit is required to proceed with a booking and secure the date.

•   All orders will only be confirmed upon payment. Payment is to be made 7 days prior to the collection date, if not deemed as cancelled.

•   We recommend that you choose your event date carefully as the deposit is non-refundable once paid. We will however do our best to accommodate a date change if changed prior to 7 days of the original order collection date.

•   Collection Method(s)
Self-collection at Pasir Ris – Complimentary
Delivery (MRT stations) – Starting from $5 onwards
Delivery charges are solely to cover our transportation fee.
Other locations will be subjected to availability.
*Do state your preferred pick-up point.

•   More details will be sent to you via email.
